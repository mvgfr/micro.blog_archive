log4j is not the problem

Dec 18, 2021

The central problem, is the enormous jenga tower that we’ve built: - Full of dependencies that virtually no one understands. - Dependencies generally chosen, for expedience. - Chosen by coders at all level of in/experience. - Driven by “ship it now”, and “move fast and break things”. - With management rarely caring about risks - until one becomes a public crisis, which can no longer be ignored.

Prediction 1: These crises will get both worse, and more frequent - bad actors (of all stripes; not only in tech), have noticed how vulnerable everything is.

Prediction 2: Even still, there will be little fundamental change in how we write & deploy code - because most of the people making these decisions haven’t felt it. Yet.

Prediction 3: The “powers that be” think they’re insulated from the pain. It may not be long til they find out how wrong they are.
